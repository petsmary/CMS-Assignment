Clubhouse Facilities

Gym Room

Located at 1/F and 2/F, providing a wide range of free-weights and modern exercise machines.

Monday to Friday: 10:00 - 22:00.

Saturday Sunday and Public Holiday: 08:00 - 22:00.

Study Rooms

7 study rooms on 3/F with 50 seats, providing a quite studying place to students.

Monday to Friday: 09:00 - 22:00.

Saturday Sunday and Public Holiday: 09:00 - 17:00.

Sauna Rooms

Located at 1/F with using the highest quality, hand-selected wood for our sauna rooms and interiors.

Monday to Friday: 10:00 - 22:00.

Saturday Sunday and Public Holiday: 08:00 - 22:00.

Swimming Pool

Located on G/F with Main pool (50 meters long, wide 25 meters (8 swimming line), water depth of 1.4 meters -1.93 meters),
Diving pool (water depth of 4.5 meters),
Amusement pool (water depth 0.5-1.5 m),
and Water ladder.

Open on everyday in 3 sessions:

06:30 – 12:00, 13:00 – 18:00, and 19:00 – 22:00.

BBQ

Located at G/F and 5/F which can host a small family reunion, a birthday party, or even a business event.

Monday to Friday: 13:00 - 23:00.

Saturday Sunday and Public Holiday: 10:00 - 24:00.
